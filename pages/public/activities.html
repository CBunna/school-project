Activities in Beskydy

Discover all year-round adventures

🥾 Mountain Hiking

More than 750 miles of marked hiking trails for all difficulty levels. Walk the ridges, visit peaks or choose easy family trails through valleys.

  • Marked trails of all colors
  • Nature trails
  • Lookout towers with views
  • Mountain huts

Season: May - October

Difficulty: Easy to challenging

🚵 Mountain Biking

Hundreds of miles of bike paths and singletracks for road and mountain bikes. From family trips to adrenaline downhills.

  • Marked bike routes
  • Bikepark Malenovice
  • E-bike rentals
  • Service points

Season: April - October

Difficulty: Easy to extreme

🏊 Water Activities

Recreational dams, aqua parks and natural swimming pools for refreshment in hot summer days.

  • Šance Dam
  • Aquapark Frenštát
  • Natural swimming pools
  • Swimming and paddleboarding

Season: June - August

Difficulty: Easy
