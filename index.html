About Beskydy

Beskydy is the easternmost mountain range in the Czech Republic covering approximately 3,000 km². The region offers beautiful nature, rich culture and excellent conditions for hiking and recreation.

Why Visit Beskydy?

Beskydy is an ideal destination for everyone seeking a combination of natural beauty, cultural richness and active recreation.

  • Beautiful mountain scenery and nature reserves
  • Extensive network of hiking trails and bike paths
  • Rich folk culture and traditions
  • Excellent local gastronomy
  • Year-round tourist activities
  • Easy access from Ostrava (25 miles)
